🖐 How to Play
  • 1Click Enable Camera and allow browser access.
  • 2Place your hand(s) in view of the camera — above a table or flat surface.
  • 3Each fingertip detected maps to a piano key left-to-right across the frame.
  • 4Tap or lower a finger toward the surface — when a fingertip reaches the lower zone, the note plays.
  • 5Use Octave +/− to shift keys. Adjust volume freely.

📱 Use Your Phone as Camera

Since your PC doesn't have a camera, here are 3 easy ways to use your phone camera in the browser:


Method 1 — Same WiFi (Easiest)
  1. Install DroidCam (Android) or EpocCam (iPhone) on your phone.
  2. Install the matching PC client/driver on your computer.
  3. Connect both to the same WiFi — your phone camera appears as a webcam in the browser.

Method 2 — USB Cable
  1. Use DroidCam with USB tethering (Android) or EpocCam with USB (iPhone).
  2. No WiFi needed — very low latency.

Method 3 — Open on Phone Directly

Open this HTML file directly on your phone browser. Your phone's front camera will work instantly — no setup needed!
